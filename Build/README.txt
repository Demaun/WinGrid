WinGrid: Replacement for Win+Up/Down/Left/Right hotkeys, with configuration and such.

Win+Esc: Opens configration window

Win+U/D/L/R: 
	Aligned? Move a window to the adjacent sector.
	Unaligned? Move a window to the closest sector, and fill the screen on the alternate axis.

Win+Alt+U/D/L/R: 
	Expand to the adjacent sector.

Win+Alt+Shift+U/D/L/R:
	Contract in the given direction.
